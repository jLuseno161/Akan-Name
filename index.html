Want to know your Akan Name?

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. In Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Day Born Male Names Female Names
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
